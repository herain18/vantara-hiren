A second chance for wildlife in need

An animal welfare initiative, Vantara is driven by two trusts: the Radhe Krishna Temple Elephant Welfare Trust (RKTEWT) and the Greens Zoological Rescue and Rehabilitation Centre (GZRRC).

Radhe Krishna Temple Elephant Welfare (RKTEW)

A safe haven for rescued elephants, RKTEWT fosters a dignified life for these gentle giants. Here, at Vantara, they can find the care, companionship, and social interaction they deserve.

Know more

Greens Zoological Rescue and Rehabilitation Centre (GZRRC)

A second chance for endangered species, GZRRC rescues endangered species, heals them, and helps them return home, while advancing science and inspiring conservation.
